How to Order & Canteen Rules
Clear system so students get food fast and fairly.
Important info
Ordering System
Follow these simple steps:
  1. 1 Join the correct queue. Separate lines for junior and senior classes to keep it fair.
  2. 2 Decide your order before reaching the counter. Check this website or the menu board.
  3. 3 Pay in cash or prepaid card. Exact change helps speed up the line.
  4. 4 Take food, move away from the counter. Let the next student come forward.
Allergy & Health: If you have any food allergy (egg, milk, nuts, etc.) inform your class teacher and canteen staff. Ask before buying.
